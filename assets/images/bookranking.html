The Best Books According To Me

My top 3 book series of all-time

Six of Crows

What can I say? Everything about this book is perfect. The characters, the plot, the worldbuilding... Plus there are multiples POVs, making the stories richer overall!

Percy Jackson

The author's writing style is really enjoyable for me. He uses a bunch of Internet references, and not to mention the amount of comedy breaks he puts, everything about this series is amazing.

Harry Potter

This series is enchanting. Every adventure Harry takes makes me feel like I am in that world. The characters feel real and I feel unreal whenever I am on that adventure.
